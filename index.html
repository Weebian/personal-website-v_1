Why The Name "Weebian"?

Weebian is my GitHub username, which is forced onto the URL of the website.

The "Weeb" part from my username is short for for Weeaboo, where Weeaboo is a mostly derogatory slang term for a Western person who is obsessed with Japanese culture.
I am not obsessed, but I believe that people should not be shamed for having a harmless interest.
Such a word lose its' negativity if people wear it despit the shunning from others.

The last part of the username "-ian" comes from my love of ancient western history; where people had interesting endings to their names.
